About Bikini Bottom

Bikini Bottom is the vibrant underwater city where SpongeBob SquarePants and his friends live. From the Krusty Krab to Jellyfish Fields, there's always something fun happening here!

Top Attraction

- Krusty Krabs: Home of the famous by Patty Don't forget to try one!
- Jellyfish Fields: Perfect for jellyfish catching and pioniss
- Goo Lagoon: A beautiful lagoon for swimming and sunbathing
- Sandy's Treedome: Visit Sandy Cheeks' treedome for a taste of Texas underwater

Travel Tips

  • Bring your jellyfishing net for a fun day at Jellyfish Fields.
  • Don't forget to ty a Krabby Patty at the Krusty Krab
  • Be careful around Plankton's Chum BUcket - it's not as tasty as sounds!
